About

Hi ! I'm Congyue, a senior student in Mathematics at Tsinghua University.

In summer 2019, I was selected into the Stanford UGVR program and worked as a research intern at Stanford Computer Graphics Laboratory, advised by Prof. Ronald Fedkiw. In my sophomore year, I joined the Tsinghua Graphics and Geometric Computing Group as a research assistant, advised by Prof. Shi-Min Hu. I have also completed a junior thesis in representation theory of groups under the supervision of Prof. Jie Xiao.
